03 — Why GottaGO

Three Structural Moats

01

Data Network Effects

Every payout creates a labelled training record: trigger event, GPS, income loss, claim outcome. After 6 months with 10,000 workers, GottaGO holds the most accurate zone-level income-loss dataset in India. No public dataset provides this. Competitors start at zero.

02

Personalised Pricing Lock-In

Workers with 12+ weeks of history get the most personalised premium. Claim-free workers accumulate a 5% cumulative discount that resets to zero if they switch. A worker at 97/week restarts at 159/week with a new provider. Retention is structural.

03

Regulatory First-Mover

IRDAI sandbox approval takes 6-12 months. Filing first occupies the regulatory slot for parametric gig-worker income protection. Neither Digit nor Acko has filed this product category. A regulatory head start cannot be shortened by budget.

Acko, Digit, Onsurity, and Toffee cover zero rupees of weather-caused income loss for gig workers.
